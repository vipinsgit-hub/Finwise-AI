# 📊 FinWise AI  
**Your personal AI-powered money, savings, and habit assistant.**

FinWise AI helps students and young adults take control of their money through
smart expense tracking, automatic budgeting, financial insights, and an AI
mindset coach that guides you toward better money habits.

---

## 🎯 What FinWise AI Will Do
- Smart expense tracking  
- AI-powered budgeting  
- Savings recommendations  
- Cashflow prediction  
- Habit & mindset coaching  
- Student-friendly money tools 


---

## 🚀 Features

### 🔹 1. Smart Expense Tracking
- Add expenses manually (v0)
- Auto-categorization (AI-powered in v1)
- Daily/weekly/monthly spending summary
- Warns you when you're overspending

### 🔹 2. AI Budget Builder
- Generates a budget based on your income & habits  
- Adjusts monthly based on spending patterns  
- Savings suggestions

### 🔹 3. Savings Automation
- Weekly saving goals  
- Round-up savings  
- “No Spend Challenge” with AI reminders  

### 🔹 4. Cashflow Prediction
- Predicts when you might run out of money  
- Sends alerts before it happens  

### 🔹 5. Debt Advisor
- Loan/EMI tracking  
- Smart repayment recommendations  

### 🔹 6. Habit & Mindset Coach
- AI tips to reduce impulse spending  
- Daily reminders  
- Motivation for consistent money habits  

### 🔹 7. Student-Focused Tools
- Canteen & daily travel tracker  
- Small-expense logging (₹10–₹50)  
- Split bills (v1+)  
- College event expense tracker

### 🔹 8. Analytics Dashboard
- Category-based charts  
- Spending heatmap  
- Savings growth view  
- Month-on-month comparison
